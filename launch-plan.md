# ScopeMint Launch Plan

## Positioning

ScopeMint helps freelancers and agencies stop losing money on bad quotes. The fastest wedge is not "AI for everyone." It is:

> Price client projects faster, protect your margin, and send tighter proposals.

That message is legible, painful, and tied to revenue.

## Best initial customer

- Freelance designers
- Webflow/Framer studios
- Small product agencies
- Solo developers building client MVPs

These people already buy tools and feel underpricing pain directly.

## Fast distribution channels

1. Post teardown threads on X and LinkedIn showing how one bad quote loses thousands.
2. DM small agencies with a live sample quote generated from their public service page.
3. Share in freelancer and agency communities with a free calculator hook.
4. Offer 10 founding-user spots with hands-on onboarding and collect objection notes.

## First monetization path

1. Offer a 7-day free trial.
2. Charge for saved quotes, branded exports, and reusable pricing presets.
3. Manually onboard the first customers and use feedback to improve the engine.

## Next product additions

1. Stripe Checkout and account creation
2. Saved client profiles and quote history
3. PDF proposal export
4. AI rewrite for client-safe wording
5. Shareable quote links
6. CRM integrations

## What to measure

- Visitor to quote-generation rate
- Quote-generation to signup rate
- Trial to paid conversion
- Most-used project types
- Average recommended quote size

## Practical sales angle

If ScopeMint helps a freelancer increase even one project quote by $500 to $2,000, paying $19 to $49 per month feels trivial. That is the core value story to lead with.
